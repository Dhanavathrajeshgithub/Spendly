Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 6, 2026

Spendly is built to help you track your personal expenses, and we take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

1. Data We Collect

When you use Spendly, we collect the information needed to provide the service. This includes:

  • Account information — your name, email address, and password (stored as a one-way hash) when you create an account.
  • Expense data — the amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions you choose to log.
  • Usage data — basic information about how you interact with the service, such as pages visited and features used, to help us improve Spendly.
  • Device data — limited technical information like browser type and IP address, used for security and diagnostics.

We do not knowingly collect any financial account credentials, bank logins, or payment card numbers. Spendly is a manual tracking tool, not a financial aggregator.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the data we collect to:

  • Operate, maintain, and improve the Spendly service.
  • Authenticate you and keep your account secure.
  • Generate the spending insights and summaries you request.
  • Respond to your questions and support requests.
  • Detect, prevent, and address abuse, fraud, or technical issues.

We will never sell your personal data to third parties, and we do not use your expense entries to train advertising profiles.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on secure servers using industry-standard encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS). Access to your account is protected by your password; you are responsible for choosing a strong password and keeping your login credentials confidential.

While we take reasonable measures to safeguard your information, no system is completely secure. If we ever become aware of a breach affecting your data, we will notify you in accordance with applicable law.

You can view, export, or delete your expense data at any time from your account settings. Deleting your account permanently removes your personal data from our active systems, subject to any retention we are required to keep for legal or security purposes.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly relies on a small set of trusted third-party providers to operate, such as hosting infrastructure and email delivery for account notifications. These providers only receive the information needed to perform their service and are bound by confidentiality and data-protection obligations.

We do not embed third-party advertising or analytics trackers that profile you across other websites. If we ever add optional analytics to improve Spendly, we will do so in a privacy-respecting way and update this policy to reflect it.

5. Your Choices and Rights

You can always:

  • Update your account information from the settings page.
  • Export a copy of your expense data.
  • Delete individual expenses or your entire account.
  • Contact us to ask questions about how your data is handled.

Depending on where you live, you may also have additional rights under laws such as the GDPR or the India Digital Personal Data Protection Act — including the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal data. We honor these requests regardless of jurisdiction where feasible.

6. Children's Privacy

Spendly is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has created an account, please contact us and we will delete it.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. If a change is material, we will let you know through the service before it takes effect. Your continued use of Spendly after the update constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out to us through the support channels listed in the app. We aim to respond to all privacy requests promptly.
